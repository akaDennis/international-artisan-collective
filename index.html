Forever Flowers — a social enterprise

Photography coming soon — add images to Pictures/ to replace this placeholder.

A beautiful story sits behind these crocheted forever flowers. What looks like a simple craft stall is in fact a social enterprise supporting people with disabilities.

  • Artist Jiaojiao is a handicapped new mom who has been crocheting for years.
  • Artist Yang is a grandmother supporting her mentally handicapped grandson; she has assembled a team of others in need and teaches them her crochet art.

Flowers are $10 per stem. Customers can pick individual stems to be wrapped into bouquets. This is more than craft — it’s a pathway to income and skills training for people with disabilities and those in need.
